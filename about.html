01 // The Jack & Jill Legacy

A Tradition of Excellence

For decades, Jack & Jill School has stood as a beacon of academic rigor and moral fortitude in Kitale, Kenya. We do not just teach students; we mold future leaders, thinkers, and global citizens equipped to navigate an ever-changing world with integrity and grace.

Our approach intertwines cutting-edge pedagogy with timeless Christian values, ensuring that every child who walks through our gates steps out into the world absolutely unparalleled.

02 // The Journey

Our Rich History

From our humble beginnings to an institution of national prestige.

The Foundation

A Vision Realized

Founded with a modest cohort of 20 students, the school was established to provide unparalleled quality education in the heart of Kitale.

Rapid Expansion

The New School

Responding to overwhelming demand from parents, Jack & Jill escalated its operations, unveiling a state-of-the-art school complete with advanced laboratories and sporting complexes.

Modern Era

National Excellence

Consistently ranking among the top institutions nationwide, producing alumni who conquer top-tier universities globally.

VISION

To be the premier educational institution empowering holistic, globally competitive leaders.

MISSION

To deliver world-class, character-driven education within a nurturing, state-of-the-art environment.
