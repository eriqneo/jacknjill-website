School Director

Mr. Pinto Nelson Atudo

Advocate of the High Court of Kenya | Passionate Educator | Child Development Enthusiast Mr. Pinto Nelson Atudo brings legal expertise and educational leadership to Jack and Jill School. As an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya, he embodies integrity, discipline, and justice but his true calling is in education with a passion for excellence shines.

He is deeply committed to nurturing children from their earliest years, through formative stages of growth to confident young adults. His love for children is evident in engagement with them in the pursuit of knowledge.

At Jack and Jill School, Mr. Atudo champions a holistic approach to private schooling balancing academic rigor with character development. His vision is to create an environment where education is not just about learning, but about shaping future leaders with compassion, resilience, and excellence.
