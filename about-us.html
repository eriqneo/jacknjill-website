01 // The Jack & Jill School Legacy

A Tradition of Excellence

For decades, Jack & Jill School has stood as a beacon of academic rigor and moral fortitude in Kitale, Kenya. We do not just teach students; we mold future leaders, thinkers, and global citizens equipped to navigate an ever-changing world with integrity and grace.

Our approach intertwines cutting-edge pedagogy with timeless Christian values, ensuring that every child who walks through our gates steps out into the world absolutely unparalleled.

Tradition of Academic Excellence
02 // The Journey

Our Rich History

From our humble beginnings to an institution of national prestige.

The Foundation

A Vision Realized

Founded with a modest cohort of 20 students, Jack & Jill school was established to provide unparalleled quality education in the heart of Kitale.

Rapid Expansion

The New School

Responding to overwhelming demand from parents, jack & Jill School escalated its operations, unveiling a state-of-the-art school complete with advanced laboratories and sporting complexes.

Modern Era

National Excellence

Consistently ranking among the top institutions nationwide, producing alumni who conquer top-tier universities globally.

OUR VISION

To be a school of excellence guided by strong moral principles.

OUR MISSION

jack & Jill School's mission is to create a safe environment where all students are nurtured to achieve academic excellence and holistic development.

Through a child-centered framework and grounded in best practices, every student will develop the confidence, knowledge, and skill set to succeed in high school and beyond.

04 // Core Principles

School Values

We PRIDE ourselves for the following Values:

Leadership

Leading one another to servant leadership in pursuit of raising God-fearing and true leaders of the future.

Learning

Treasuring intellectual growth by keenly noting different needs, creativity, and talent.

Diversity

Enabling a wide range of activities to help in raising each learner's God-given talent and skills.

Integrity

Honesty, respect, and courage guide our code of behaviour in attaining excellence.
